# The AI Manifesto

## Using AI responsibly in Development and Collaboration

Guiding responsible AI in development and collaboration.

Version 1.0

---

## Why this matters

AI gives us superpowers. But superpowers need principles. This manifesto is a set
of guidelines for using AI tools — LLMs, code generators, assistants — responsibly
in development and collaboration. It centres human insight, accountability, and
learning. Inspired by Daniel Roe's post "Using AI in open source."

## The 5 Principles

**1. Speak in your own voice.**
Use AI to sharpen your thinking, but keep your actions authentic.
Never let an LLM speak for you.

**2. Own the strategy.**
Let AI explore options and test your assumptions, but you remain the architect
of your work. Be aware of bias and mistakes.
Never let an LLM think for you.

**3. Take absolute responsibility for the final result.**
Never deflect blame by saying "The AI said so." Credit and accountability
remain with you.
Never let an LLM own your work.

**4. AI helps you learn and investigate further.**
Treat it as a partner, not as an oracle that ends the exploration.
Never let an LLM replace your curiosity.

**5. AI should empower people and reduce barriers.**
Foster inclusion rather than creating new forms of gatekeeping.
Never let an LLM discourage someone else.
